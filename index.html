About
Early life and education

Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton was born on 7 January 1985 in Stevenage, Hertfordshire. His father, Anthony Hamilton, is of Grenadian descent, while his mother, Carmen Larbalestier, is British, from Birmingham, making him mixed-race;Hamilton's parents separated when he was two, after which he lived with his mother and older half-sisters, Samantha and Nicola, until he was twelve.[10] Hamilton then lived with his father, stepmother Linda, and his half-brother Nicolas, who is also a professional racing driver. Hamilton was raised a Catholic.

Formula One career
Lewis Hamilton, a British racing driver, is widely regarded as one of the greatest in Formula One history. He debuted in 2007 with McLaren and won his first World Championship in 2008, becoming the youngest ever at the time. Hamilton joined Mercedes in 2013, where he dominated the sport, clinching seven World Championships (2008, 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020), equaling Michael Schumacher's record. Known for his exceptional skill, consistency, and speed, Hamilton holds numerous records, including the most wins, pole positions, and podium finishes. Beyond racing, he is an advocate for diversity, environmental sustainability, and social justice.
Driver profile
Lewis Hamilton, a British racing driver, is a seven-time Formula 1 World Champion, tying Michael Schumacher for the most titles. Born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England, Hamilton began karting at a young age and quickly rose through the ranks. He debuted in F1 with McLaren in 2007, winning his first championship in 2008. In 2013, he joined Mercedes-AMG Petronas, where he dominated the sport. Known for his exceptional skill, consistency, and determination, Hamilton is also a vocal advocate for diversity and environmental sustainability. Off the track, he is passionate about fashion, music, and philanthropy.
Personal life
Lewis Hamilton, a renowned Formula 1 driver, was born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, Hertfordshire, England. His parents, Carmen Larbalestier and Anthony Hamilton, separated when he was two. Raised by his mother until twelve, he then lived with his father and stepmother. Anthony played a crucial role in his early racing career, managing him until 2010. Hamilton is known for his close relationship with his half-brother Nicolas, who also pursued racing despite having cerebral palsy. Beyond racing, Hamilton is a passionate advocate for animal rights, environmental issues, and diversity in motorsports, often using his platform to promote these causes.
Honours and achievements
Lewis Hamilton, a British Formula 1 driver, has achieved unparalleled success in motorsport. He has won seven World Drivers' Championship titles, equaling Michael Schumacher's record. Hamilton holds the record for the most pole positions and race wins, surpassing 100 victories. Known for his exceptional driving skills, he has secured numerous podium finishes and fastest laps. Beyond F1, Hamilton was knighted in 2021 for his services to motorsport. He has also been awarded the BBC Sports Personality of the Year multiple times. Off-track, Hamilton is a vocal advocate for diversity and environmental sustainability in racing and beyond.
